Your next "go-to" collaboration application!

Recruit is a platform where freelancers and indie developers can explore opportunities, build up experiences, and make new connections.

FEATURES

Personal Dashboard

Organize your space! Keep track of your own posts and the posts that you have saved or followed to receive notifications for.

Collaborate with Others

Make connections with the people you work with! Comment on project threads to express your interest. Coordinate deadlines and make plans with other users with built-in messaging.

Filter Search

Look for opportunities that fit your skillset and needs! Visually descriptive filters help you sort through threads and eventually find a project that really speaks to you.

Host Your Portfolio

A portfolio is all about who you are and what you do! Recruit's project-based profile page lets you showcase your projects, process, and whom you have collaborated with to grow your skills.

Come and Chat With Us

If you have any questions or concerns, or just want to say hi!
