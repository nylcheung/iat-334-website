Case Study Process

Domain Problem

Freelancers and independent developers looking to gain experience or start their own projects related to gaming struggle because of their inexperience, lack of connections, or because they do not have all the skills to create what they envisioned.


Initial Research

From our research, we found that there were no apps or platforms that were specifically for freelancers or indie developers to collaborate with others on creative projects. For these individuals that start their own projects, it is often a labor of love that that is difficult to do alone, requiring long periods of commitment and a variety of skills to finish.

Proposal

With our app, we aim to provide a platform for our users to:

  • Explore different opportunities through a search page with clear visual hierarchy to reduce cognitive load and make content easily scannable.
  • Build connections with others whether it is by expressing interest in someone's project or showing your portfolio.
  • Feel at ease with Recruit's casual environment and not feel intimidated by their prospects.
